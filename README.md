# DungeonGod

Simple 2D top-down roguelike project in C++17 with SFML.
The entire game world consists of **7 interconnected rooms**, structured as a progression of increasingly difficult arenas:

* **Room 1 (The Safe Haven):** A starting zone free of danger, allowing the player to test controls and prepare for the run.
* **Rooms 2–6 (The Geometric Gauntlet):** Action-packed combat arenas spawning waves of unique, geometrically-shaped enemies (Squares, Triangles, and more) that must be completely cleared to unlock the doors.
* **Room 7 (The Final Showdown):** The deepest layer of the dungeon, home to **The Boss**—the ultimate test of your reflex and resource management.

## Project structure
